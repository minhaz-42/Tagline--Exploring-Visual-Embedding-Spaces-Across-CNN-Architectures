CNN Architectures Compared

Pre-trained on ImageNet, evaluated on Caltech-101

ResNet-101

2048-d

Deep residual network with 101 layers and skip connections. Features extracted from global average pooling achieve state-of-the-art retrieval performance.

ZFNet

4096-d

Zeiler & Fergus network with deconvolution-based visualization. High-dimensional 4096-d embeddings from the FC-7 layer capture rich semantic information.

GoogLeNet

1024-d

Inception v1 architecture with parallel multi-scale convolutions. The most compact 1024-d embedding balances efficiency with strong retrieval quality.
